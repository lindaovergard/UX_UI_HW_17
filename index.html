Hei!

This is Linda Overgård.

Designer of User Experiences and Graphic Designer with a special place in my heart for typography. I’m from Norway, and now I’m based outside Washington DC.

I approach the design process with patience and curiosity, and love problem solving.

What drives me is the constant opportunity to learn and to get to know people. By utilizing a variety of research methods I dive deeper into my users needs and identify their pain points. This information allows me to create and iterate tools and experiences that are personal and effortless.
